## Plinko Overview

This audit evaluates the Plinko game operated by Duel Casino under the ProvablyFair.org Audit Framework v1.0.
Plinko is a prediction game where a ball drops through a board of pegs and lands in a slot. The outcome is determined by the path taken, which maps to a multiplier based on risk level and row configuration.

<SectionHeading label="Context" title="Purpose" />

This audit determines whether outcomes are cryptographically reproducible, statistically fair, and resistant to
manipulation by either players or the casino.

input them into an independent verifier, and get the exact same outcome as the live game, the casino could not have
altered the result.
